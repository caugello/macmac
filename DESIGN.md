Design System: Terracotta Light

Brand Persona

MacMac is a practical, warm, and family-oriented kitchen tool. The "Terracotta Light" theme evokes a "morning-in-the-kitchen" feel—bright, airy, and inviting, while maintaining professional utility.

Color Palette

Core Colors

Primary (Terracotta): #d66853 - Used for primary actions, active states, and brand highlights.

Surface: #fcf9f8 - The main background color, providing a clean and soft canvas.

On-Surface: #1f1b1a - High-contrast
